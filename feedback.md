Feedback & Support  
===  

Sprout Diary is a baby growth record app for families, focused on daily care data for children aged 0-3. It helps parents and caregivers track feeding, sleep, diaper logs, health status, and growth progress in one place.

Your feedback is very important to us. If you encounter issues, have product questions, or want to suggest features, please contact us anytime. Your input helps us continuously improve `Sprout Diary`.  

You can reach us through in-app feedback or by email at **kennyiseeyou@gmail.com**. We do our best to respond quickly and provide practical support. Thank you for choosing Sprout Diary.
